## INTEGRATION STRATEGY

### **Books Completed:** 1/10
### **Next Priority:** Books 2-4 (Sincero, Brown, Burns)

### **Process for Each Book:**
1. Upload summary/transcript/key excerpts
2. Extract 3-5 core concepts
3. Map to your personal story themes
4. Identify integration points
5. Flag most resonant ideas

### **Your Story Themes to Map:**
- Performance vs. authenticity
- Approval-seeking and worthiness
- Religious/family identity theft
- Shame and secrecy
- Leaving to find yourself
- Responsibility vs. fault
- Boundaries and saying "no"
- Death of old identity/birth of true self
